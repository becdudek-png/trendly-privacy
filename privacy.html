Trendly - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Hi, I'm Bec, the independent developer behind Trendly. I built Trendly on my own, and I care about your privacy. This policy explains, in plain English, what Trendly does (and mostly doesn't do) with your information.

The short version

Trendly has no accounts and no servers of mine. I never see, collect, receive, or sell your information. Your data lives on your device, and,  if you turn on iCloud sync, in your own private iCloud account, which only you can access.

What data Trendly uses

Apple Health data you choose to share (such as sleep, steps, heart rate, energy, and body weight), read only with your permission and revocable any time in the Health app or Settings; and information you type in, like weight, calories, goals, streaks, custom logs, and notes.

What I collect about you

Nothing. Trendly has no analytics, tracking, advertising, third-party SDKs, or backend of mine. I have no way to identify you and no access to anything you log.

iCloud sync (optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your logs are stored in your own private iCloud database so they stay in step across your devices and survive replacing your phone. This data is handled by Apple under Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy, kept within your personal iCloud account, and is not visible or accessible to me. You can turn sync off, and deleting the app or managing iCloud storage removes it. If you never enable it, your data stays only on your device.

Subscriptions

Trendly offers an optional subscription (Trendly Premium). All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store, I never see your card details or payment information. Apple may share limited, aggregate sales information with me (for example, how many subscriptions were sold), which does not identify you.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

Health data accessed through HealthKit is used only to display your own trends inside the app. In line with Apple's rules, Trendly never uses Health data for advertising or marketing, and never shares it with third parties.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. They are not sent through any server of mine and contain no data about you beyond the reminder text.

Links to other services

Trendly links to external support organisations. If you tap one it opens in your browser and you leave Trendly. Those sites have their own privacy policies, which I'm not responsible for.

Your control over your data

You can edit or delete entries in the app, export your data as a spreadsheet, revoke Health access any time, and deleting the app removes its locally-stored data from your device.

Children

Trendly is intended for adults 18 and over and is not directed at children.

Not medical advice

Trendly is a general wellbeing and habit tool, not a medical device or a source of medical, nutritional, or psychological advice. Insights and estimates are approximate and may be wrong. Always consult a qualified professional about your health. You use Trendly at your own discretion and risk.

Changes to this policy

If I update this policy I'll change the "Last updated" date above and post the new version here.
